About Us

Why Seigi Likes Coffee?

Iced coffee is my go-to delight that never lets me down, which is why I like it. Whether I'm relaxing at home or hanging out with friends, there's something about the combination of cold coffee and sweetness that just feels right. Additionally, I enjoy that I can switch it up by adding various toppings or flavors based on how I'm feeling, so trying something new is always fun. Additionally, whether I have schoolwork or other tasks to complete, the caffeine boost truly helps me stay focused. I wouldn't swap it for anything, and it's more than just a drink to me it's a part of my routine.

Why Claire Likes Coffee?

Iced coffee is my favorite since it's pleasant and refreshing, especially on hot days where I crave something cold. Sharing it on TikTok or Instagram is fun because it's also really fashionable and looks fantastic in photos. Additionally, I can alter it to my liking by adding different milk or syrups, ensuring that I always feel like I'm getting a special drink. I can stay energized during school or after a long day thanks to the caffeine boost. It's also a pleasant part of my routine, and there's something about the entire coffee culture that makes getting iced coffee with friends feel like a social thing.
